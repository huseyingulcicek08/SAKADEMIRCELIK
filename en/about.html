  • 2012 - The platform part of our hot rolling mill has been modernized.
  • 2013 - Production of rounds (shafts) and hexagons that have been specially cut, chamfered, straightened-polished or hexagonal straightening has been started.
  • 2015 - New continuous groups and other additional and automation systems were put into service. Thus, more capacity, quality, variety, serial and technological production was started.

GENERAL INFORMATION

Our company, which is a family company, took its place in the Turkish industry with a small turning workshop that Mustafa Saka established in Karabük in 1955.
A short time later, in 1956, a small hot rolling mill, employing twelve people, started producing rebar.

The company, which started to be managed under the name of Mustafa Saka Varisleri after his death in 1968, was transformed into a Joint Stock Company in 1989 and renamed SAKA Demir Çelik Sanayi Ve Ticaret A.Ş..

  • 1987 - In addition to the hot rolling mill, the cold drawing unit was put into operation.
  • 1988 - Production of manufacturing steel products has started.
  • 1989 - The current production method was abandoned and sandblasting machines started to be used.
  • 1991 - All units of cutting and grinding have been commissioned.
  • 1995 - Peeling and straightening - polishing unit was commissioned.
  • 1999 - The additional building built to expand the production area was put into use.
