Our Facility

Using advanced technology, high quality products are produced in our facility. Our production capacity is approximately 100,000 tons per year, half of which is cold drawn products.

Residential

Today , the facility established on a closed area of approximately 15,000 m2 ; It consists of slab area, product area, hot and cold rolling units, machinery, welding and maintenance workshops.

Our Quality Policy

SAKA DEMİR ÇELİK A.Ş management resolutely guarantees to allocate its resources for the implementation of the Quality Management System and for the continuous improvement of its effectiveness.

  • To ensure customer satisfaction with high quality principles and to work customer-oriented.
  • To obtain strengths by managing risks and opportunities correctly.
  • To fulfill the legal and regulatory requirements and applicable conditions within the enterprise.
  • To exert maximum effort to achieve quality goals and process goals.

Quality Consciousness

  • To adopt quality awareness by our senior management and employees, to continuously improve it, and to organize trainings to contribute to the competencies and professional experiences of our employees.
  • To take the necessary measures for a clean environment.
  • To ensure continuous improvement of the quality management system.
  • It has adopted the principles of ensuring the occupational health and safety of our employees, taking the necessary measures to prevent occupational diseases, and thus allocating a peaceful and reliable working environment to our employees.
